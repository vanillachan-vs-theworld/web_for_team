Code With Impact

Code With Impact is a female led organization in which high school mentors plan and host biannual hackathons for elementary and middle school girls teaching them coding, marketing, presenting, and more!

The hackathons are based on relevant social issues like financial literacy, mental health, organization, health and fitness, environmental sustainability, food supply, agriculture, etc.
